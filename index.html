FAQs

Kitchen Remodeling Questions

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Houston?
The cost typically ranges from $10,000 to $50,000+, depending on size, materials, and scope. Basic updates run $10K–$20K, mid-range remodels $20K–$40K, and high-end remodels $40K+. We provide detailed, transparent estimates for your specific project.
How long does a kitchen remodel take?
Most projects take 3 to 8 weeks once construction begins. Timelines depend on project complexity, material availability, and structural changes. We provide a clear timeline before starting.
Do I need permits for a kitchen remodel?
In many cases, permits are required — especially for electrical work, plumbing changes, and structural modifications. Our team handles the permitting process to ensure everything is up to code.
Can I stay in my home during the remodel?
Yes, most homeowners stay in their homes during a kitchen remodel. You may need to set up a temporary kitchen during construction. We work to minimize disruption.
What adds the most value in a kitchen remodel?
The biggest value upgrades include modern cabinets, quartz or granite countertops, updated lighting, open-concept layouts, and energy-efficient appliances.
What is the best countertop material?
Popular choices in Houston include quartz (low maintenance, durable) and granite (natural look, heat resistant). Quartz is often preferred for its durability and minimal upkeep.
What flooring works best for Houston kitchens?
Due to Houston's humidity, the best options are tile (very durable, water-resistant) and luxury vinyl plank (waterproof, cost-effective).
Can you open up walls for an open-concept kitchen?
Yes! We specialize in removing walls and creating open-concept layouts, including structural modifications when needed.
