Tub to Shower Conversion

Converting your bathtub into a shower is an excellent way to maximize space and create a more accessible bathing area. This upgrade is particularly beneficial for smaller bathrooms or homeowners looking to age in place.

  • Space Efficiency: Free up valuable floor space, making your bathroom feel larger
  • Enhanced Accessibility: Eliminate high tub walls, reducing slip and fall risks
  • Modern Aesthetic: Frameless glass doors and custom tile work
  • Increased Home Value: Modern showers appeal to today's buyers

Shower Remodeling

Upgrading your shower can significantly enhance the overall look and feel of your bathroom. Whether it's replacing outdated fixtures, installing new tiles, or reconfiguring the layout, our team handles all aspects of shower remodeling.

  • Custom Tile Installation: Wide range of options for a unique space
  • Modern Fixtures: Contemporary showerheads, faucets, and controls
  • Layout Reconfiguration: Add seating or expand the space
  • Waterproofing & Sealing: Prevent water damage and mold
